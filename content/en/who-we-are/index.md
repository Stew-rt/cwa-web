## Key Members - The COMMITTEE
As with most clubs, we have a Chairman, Treasurer and Secretary who oversee the administrative aspects of the club, and who run the AGM on the first meeting in December. The AGM is also where other members of the club vote on various aspects of how the club is run, and at the relevant time elect a new Chairman, Treasurer and Secretary. A Chairman is elected every year, with the Secretary and Treasurer remaining in post for two years before being elected.
